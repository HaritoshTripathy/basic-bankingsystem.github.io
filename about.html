Welcome to Liber Bank!

Liber Bank was established in the year 1978, with an aim to provide the best services to all its customers.
Our founder, Mr.Dumbledore had this vision :

"Customers are like our God and hence we should never displease them."


With the efforts of our founder and the collective hardwork of the employees, we have successfully helped customers in and around the world. We have bought the transfer of money at the fingertips.
Net banking has been an added facility of the Liber Bank.


At Liber Bank, we do not take our employees as servers but as a team of capable individuals. We
believe in Team work and togetherness.
